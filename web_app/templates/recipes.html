Recipes Generator

Instructions

Please select the method by which you wish to narrow your search for recipes (i.e., By Category, By Nationality/Country of Origin, By Keyword, or Randomly) and then in the space box provided below the dropdown menu, please input your selection based on the method you chose. For example, if you wish to receive a list of recipes by Category, you could enter "Seafood". If you wish to receive a list of recipes by Nationality/Country of Origin, you could enter "Canadian". If you wish to receive a list of recipes by Keyword, you could enter "orange". If you wish to to receive a list of random recipes, do not enter anything in the provided box. The options listed above are not meant to be represent the only selections a user can enter, but are rather listed to show what valid input could look like. For a list of all potential valid options, please refer to the list provided on the "Help" page of this web application. The user is also to request the recipe list provided by the Recipe Generator to an email of their choosing. If you wish to receive your individual recipe list by email, please enter your email in the text box titled "Email". Once you have filled everything out with valid inputs, press the Submit button to receive your recipe list from the Recipe Generator.
